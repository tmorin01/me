About Me

I am a Senior Undergraduate student studying Cognitive & Brain Science and Computer Science with an interest in biomedical imaging. Currently, I am studying how resting state functional connectivity in the brain may be influenced by prescription opioid medications. My other interests include American Sign Language and long distance running. After I graduate, I plan to purse a PhD focused in cognitive/computational neuroscience and neuroimaging. Below, I've included some highlights from past projects that I've worked on. If you have any questions about my research, feel free to get in touch.
